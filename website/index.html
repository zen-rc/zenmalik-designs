Meet Zen!

Hello, my name is Zen (she/her). I am a Philly-based web developer with experience in event coordination, business and social sciences, who enjoys bringing a unique and versatile perspective to my clients. I have a deep understanding of business fundamentals, allowing me to tailor my approach to meet the market’s needs and create innovative solutions to their problems. As an event coordinator, I honed my skills in logistics and developed unique and creative problem solving abilities to serve my clients needs to reach and maintain an audience. My analytical skills also helped me identify areas of improvement, resulting in even more successful events. As a web developer, I am leveraging my ability to promote successful engagement to enhance businesses’ accessibility and brand awareness in the digital space. My diverse range of experiences and knowledge enables me to understand stakeholders on both a macro and micro-level and serve them with the utmost respect and dedication. I am confident in my ability to create solutions that meet the needs of all parties. (Fun fact: I am also a photographer! All pictures on this site are original.)
